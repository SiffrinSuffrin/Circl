# When to make a new symbol?
Check: 
1) Is there a series of symbols that does what your single symbol would do? If, yes, go to 1.A, else go to 2
1.A) If there is something that does it already, can you vary it for other variations of the same problem by changing a constant? ie adding 2 ("2"+) can also be an addition of three ("3"+). If there is, maybe reconsider adding it? Continue to 2
2) Is what you propose to add really complicated (ie symbol xyz would interpret malbodge or play minesweper)? If it fits this, probably a bad fit for Circl! We're looking for the middle ground of complexity, which is stuff that does complex enough to add functonality/make programmers lives easier, but not to the level of complexity where all the fun is removed. #TODO: add more details
